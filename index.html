Technoblade Never Dies

Image by AmyTheMudkip

Technoblade was a Minecraft Youtuber who sadly passed away on June 30th of 2022 due to sarcoma. It sounds a bit silly, but his conent really helped me get through some tough times, and his will to fight, no matter how bleak the odds, is something truly inspiring. I, and many others, wouldn't be on the path I am today if it weren't for him. Rest in peace king.
